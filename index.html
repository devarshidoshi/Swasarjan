Welcome to Swa Sarjan Foundation

Swa Sarjan Foundation is a Non-Profit Making Organisation, recognized under United Nations, Social and Economic Affairs, which works majorly on providing education to less privileged children while keeping youth and women empowerment in focus. Swa Sarjan team is expanded in 6 cities and 9 states of India and 13 countries worldwide and actively working on solving regional problems and everything that is leading towards a brighter future with overall development.

The name of an organization has a deep meaning driven in itself. Swa means 'Self' and Sarjan means 'Development' which collectively conveys the message of Self Development and thus being part of it is enlightening as an inclusive society of various different minds working together, exchanging ideas, and knowledge, and developing as better individuals while keeping humanitarian services at the center.

About SSF

OUR MISSION

To teach every mind which is left unshaped and groom the rationality of those youthful minds .To untangle the regional socially-ill hurdles and adding value to community regarding vivid culture. Creating a Women friendly and Youth Empowering society. Create a zero illiteracy rate .To create a 100% Educated Economy.

OUR VISION

To Enlighten each an every abandoned mind . To obtain Gender quality and uplifting deprived sections of society , furthermore inspiring youth community to work towards overall development across the growth of individual and the society.

OUR BELIEF

Not only to promote cyclopaedic education but also to create a positive thunderstorm of bold assertive personalities, righteous non bias individuals who are proficient in all spheres of life.

OUR FAITH

We embrace our values of Empathy, Integrity, Honesty and providing the most nonpareil quality education which also includes the moralities of empowering youth and women as our precedence.
